About

Hello, my name is Adam Sims and I am a 3rd year gameplay programmer studying Games Programming at the University of Lincoln.

I am mainly interested in procedural content generation and creating topdown games, though I have a keen interest in networking and I am actively trying to explore new areas of game development that interest me. Currently I am working on my dissertation which revolves around the use of procedural animation in horror games.

I also actively play video games as a hobby and I frequently take part in game jams, including Global Game Jam. I enjoy participating in game jams as they give me the opportunity to push myself and to better my skills both on my own and in a team.
